Questions landlords ask.

Can landlords deduct mileage?

Many landlords track business mileage for rental property activity, such as trips to properties, repair supplies, meetings, and tax or banking errands. The exact treatment depends on your situation, so confirm with your tax professional.

What does LandMile export?

LandMile is designed to help you export trip history in CSV format with the details you need to review and share with your CPA.

Is this only for landlords?

No. Real estate agents, property managers, home inspectors, and other real estate operators can use it too. The landing page is focused on landlords because that pain is specific and easy to understand.
