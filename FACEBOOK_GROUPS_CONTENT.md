## Post 4 — The Dollar Math Post
*Best for: Tom Ferry Community, business-focused groups*

```
ran the numbers on this yesterday and i kind of can't stop thinking about it

if you drive to 5 showings a week, average 8 miles round trip each, plus one listing appointment, one open house, and miscellaneous errands — you're probably doing 70-90 miles of deductible driving per week without even realizing it.

at the current IRS rate ($0.67/mile) that's roughly $47-60/week. $2,400-3,100/year. just from a pretty normal week.

and that's assuming you actually track it. most of us don't. which means we're basically donating money to the IRS every April.

the agents i know who are serious about this deduction say the key is automatic tracking — otherwise you forget, you reconstruct from memory, and you lose the documentation if you ever get audited.

am i the only one who didn't realize how much this adds up? what are people doing to stay on top of this?
```
